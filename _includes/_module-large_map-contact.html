Getting to Seven Points

Seven Points is located at the “Main and Main” intersection in the heart of Uptown. Those driving can park in our 730-space parking ramp, connected to the shopping center via a skyway.

Parking

For just $12, you can park all day or overnight. Take a ticket as you enter the ramp and pay on your way out. Or for faster exiting you can pay at one of the stations in the ramp or in the Seven Points building. You will have 15 minutes to exit the ramp after you have paid at the station.

Other Location Info
